FAQ

Is this a real mining service?
This MVP is a demo. The site shows how VerdiHash could sell hashpower. Integrate a payment processor and a real backend to make it production-ready.
How is hashpower measured?
Hashpower is measured in TH/s (terahashes per second). The more TH/s you own, the larger your share of the network mining rewards (after fees).
How do payouts work?
Payouts should be handled by a backend system which tracks produced shares and schedules Bitcoin transfers. This demo does not implement payouts.
